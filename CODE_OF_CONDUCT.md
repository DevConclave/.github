# Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of the DevConclave community, we are committed to creating a friendly, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone. 
We value the participation of every individual and want everyone to have a positive experience contributing to and engaging with our organization.

By participating in the DevConclave community, including but not limited to contributing to our repositories, joining discussions, or interacting with other community members, 
you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct and uphold its principles at all times.

## Expected Behavior

We expect all participants to adhere to the following principles:

- Be respectful and considerate of others' opinions, ideas, and experiences.
- Exercise empathy and avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory behavior.
- Be open to constructive feedback and willing to collaborate with others in a positive manner.
- Use inclusive language and avoid derogatory or offensive comments or language.
- Be mindful of the impact of your words and actions on others in the community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any form, including but not limited to, verbal, written, or physical abuse.
- Discrimination, prejudice, or offensive comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, religion, disability, or any other personal characteristics.
- Personal attacks or derogatory remarks targeted at individuals or groups.
- Publishing or displaying explicit or violent content.
- Engaging in any form of disruptive, harmful, or unethical behavior.

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you believe someone has violated the Code of Conduct, we urge you to report the incident promptly to the DevConclave team by c
ontacting us via [email](mailto:krapansh@gmail.com) or any other communication channel mentioned in our organization's README. All reports will be kept confidential.

DevConclave administrators will review and investigate all reported incidents. In the event of a violation, appropriate action will be taken, which may include issuing warnings, 
temporary or permanent bans from the community, or other measures deemed necessary.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all participants in the DevConclave community, both online and offline, including but not limited to:

- GitHub repositories
- Communication channels (e.g., Discord, forums, mailing lists)
- Social media platforms
- Events, meetups, conferences, and any other community gatherings
